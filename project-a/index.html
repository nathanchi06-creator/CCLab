Dreamcatcher

Scientific Name:

Somnium captor digitalis

Discovered by / at:

In Nate's Sleep: March 8, 2026

Family:

Somnium captor digitalis is a dreamcatcher creature that floats through space collecting drifting dreams. It is believed that these creatures once traveled together in groups, forming large dreamcatcher webs that helped them gather energy from floating dream particles. However, this particular creature appears to be alone. It moves through space searching for signals, hoping one day it might find others of its kind again...

Habitat:

Somnium captor digitalis lives in a dark and quiet space filled with stars, drifting dream particles, and large web-like structures that resemble dreamcatchers. It likes places where it can move freely and slowly through the environment. These webs help trap small dream fragments that float through space, which the creature can sense and collect as food.

Appearance:

Somnium captor digitalis has a soft, floating body that slowly expands and shrinks as it moves, almost like it is breathing. The creature drifts through space in a smooth, swimming motion, as if space were an ocean. Long strands extend from its body and trail behind it like threads from a dreamcatcher, gently waving as it moves. These strands help it sense the movement of dream particles around it. Its colors are usually soft purples and blues, which reflect the calm but lonely environment it lives in. Sometimes the colors change slightly when the creature reacts to something in its environment. At the center of its body is a large eye that watches the space around it and follows signals or movement. Because its body constantly stretches, pulses, and shifts, the creature always looks slightly different, making it feel alive rather than static.

Key Characteristics:

  • -Moves through space like it is swimming slowly in zero gravity
  • -Its body gently pulses and expands, almost like breathing
  • -Reacts to movement and signals in its environment
  • -Long strands trail behind it and move like threads of a dreamcatcher

What we know so far about Dreamcatcher? Dreamcatchers originally come from Indigenous North American traditions. They are objects made with a circular frame and woven web that are believed to catch bad dreams while letting good dreams pass through. The design of this creature was inspired by that idea. The dreamcatcher web in its environment traps drifting dream particles, which the creature collects and interacts with.
Humans should interact with Somnium captor digitalis carefully and calmly. The creature reacts to signals and movement, so sudden actions may startle it. It is best to observe it slowly and allow it to move freely through its environment. Light or small signals can guide the creature’s attention, but disturbing its dreamcatcher web too much may disrupt its ability to collect dream particles.
